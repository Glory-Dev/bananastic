Bananastic

A startup project that aims to achieve sustainability and responsible production of the packaging industry, by offering eco-friendly packaging materials manufactured of banana tree wastes. Our products ideally equip the marketplace needs by offering the premium quality, cost effectiveness, and successively obtaining the ability of acting as fertilizers after usage, which gives us an edge beyond our competitors.
